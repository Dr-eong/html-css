INTRO

Tumbler is an insulated mug or cup that you can take on the go. They can be made of a variety of materials, like glass, plastic and stainless steel. Stainless steel is popular because it’s great at controlling temperature. While water bottles are good for holding water, tumblers are more specialized. They can feature sip lids and handles! A standard water bottle will keep your drink contained, but it’s not great for keeping liquids hot or cold. Tumblers can do both, making sure that your beverage stays at the right temperature.
